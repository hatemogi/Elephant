# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 12, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how this app handles information when you use the game.

This game is a puzzle game where a baby elephant moves crates, opens banana boxes, and solves stages. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little data as possible.

## Information We Collect

At this time, the app does **not directly collect personal information** such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Precise location
- Photos
- Contacts
- Health data

The app is designed to work without requiring you to create an account.

## Game Progress and Storage

The app stores gameplay-related information in order to provide core features, such as:

- Stage progress
- World unlock status
- Settings preferences
- Sound or control preferences

This information may be stored locally on your device.

To help keep your progress in sync across your Apple devices, the app may also use **Apple iCloud key-value storage**. This allows certain gameplay data and app preferences to be synchronized through your iCloud account.

The synced data is limited to app-related progress and settings, and is used only to support the gameplay experience.

## iCloud Sync

If iCloud is enabled on your device and available for your Apple account, some app data may be synchronized using Apple’s iCloud services.

We do not access your iCloud account directly. iCloud synchronization is provided and managed by Apple. Please refer to Apple’s privacy documentation for more information about how Apple handles iCloud data.

## In-App Purchases

The app may offer optional paid content, such as additional worlds or stage packs, through Apple’s In-App Purchase system.

When you make a purchase, payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive your full payment information, such as your credit card number.

Please refer to Apple’s privacy documentation for details about how Apple processes purchase-related information.

## Third-Party Services

The app may rely on Apple-provided services to support certain features, including:

- App Store
- In-App Purchases
- iCloud
- TestFlight
- Device-based app functionality

If Apple services are used, Apple may process certain information under its own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Apple’s Privacy Policy for more information.

## Analytics and Tracking

This app does **not use third-party advertising SDKs** and does **not track you across apps or websites** for advertising purposes.

If analytics or crash reporting tools are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

## Children’s Privacy

This game is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe personal information from a child has been provided to us in error, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data Retention

Because the app currently does not directly collect personal information on its own servers, we generally do not retain personal data ourselves.

Gameplay progress and settings may remain stored on your device and, if iCloud sync is enabled, may also remain in your iCloud storage according to Apple’s systems and retention practices.

You may remove locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device. iCloud-synced data may also be removed according to Apple’s account and storage controls.

## Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights related to your personal data. Since the app currently does not directly collect or store personal information on its own servers, many of these rights may not apply in practice.

If you have a privacy-related question, you may contact us using the information below.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, or platform features.

When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
