# Elephant

Elephant in Ching-mai

## Welcome 

Welcome to a charming Sokoban-style puzzle adventure starring a baby elephant and banana-filled crates!

## Solve bite-sized puzzles

Move smart, push crates, and find the perfect path. Each stage is designed to be quick to play, yet satisfying to solve.

## Play your way

	• Swipe anywhere to move one tile at a time
	• Drag from the elephant to follow a path and move multiple tiles in one go
	• Undo anytime — try strategies without stress
	• Restart the stage whenever you want a fresh attempt

If you love thoughtful puzzles with a warm, playful vibe—this adventure is for you.
